Short Bio

Yingwei Li is a 2nd-year PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Dr. Alan Yuille. His research interests are computer vision and machine learning. He is a member of Computational Cognition, Vision, and Learning.

Before that, he obtained B.S. in Computer Science at Fudan University in 2018. He visited Johns Hopkins University in 2018 as a visiting undergraduate hosted by Dr. Alan Yuille. He also visited National Taiwan University as an exchange student.
